About Emerson Black

Emerson Black is a young adult mystery novelist who loves to explore the dark corners of the human psyche. Their stories are filled with suspense, intrigue, and a touch of the supernatural. With a background in psychology and a passion for storytelling, Emerson crafts intricate plots and compelling characters that keep readers on the edge of their seats.

Born and raised in a small town with a dark history, Emerson draws inspiration from the eerie atmosphere and unsettling tales that surrounded their childhood. They believe in the power of stories to illuminate the hidden aspects of human nature and to confront the shadows that lurk within us all.

When not writing, Emerson enjoys exploring abandoned buildings, researching local legends, and spending time with their pet raven, Poe.
